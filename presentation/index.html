Color Theory

A body of practical guidance to color mixing and the visual effects of a specific color combination.

Colors...

  •   Contribute to an emotion or feeling
  •   Are related to one another
  •   Contribute to the usability of an application

“Warm” vs “Cool”

“Warm” Colors

  •   Red, Orange, Yellow, ...
  •   Stimulating
  •   Active

“Cool” Colors

  •   Blue, Gray, Violet, ...
  •   Calm
  •   Passive

Also, each hue has emotional associations.

Red
Energy. Passion. Intensity.
Yellow
Attention. Light. Intellect.
Blue
Trust. Loyalty. Peace.
Green
Organic. Freshness. Safety.
Orange
Festive. Cautious. Radiance.
Violet
Wealth. Darkness. Royalty.
